---
title: "Blacklisting a Client"
description: "Learn how to blacklist a client by adding their email to the restricted list. Prevent unwanted bookings while maintaining control over your client database."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1">
    ## Navigate to the Clients Section

    Click “[Clients](http://app.heyallset.com/clients)” in the side menu to access the client management dashboard. This section contains all registered customers, their contact details, booking history, and management options.

    ![images/adding-new-clients.png](/images/adding-new-clients.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2">
    ## Click the Blacklist Icon

    At the top middle of the Clients section, click the Blacklist icon to open the blacklist management page.

    ![images/cick-blacklist.png](/images/cick-blacklist.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3">
    ## Enter Client Email

    In the provided field, enter the email address of the client you want to blacklist. This ensures that the system prevents future bookings or purchases associated with this email.

    ![images/blacklist-email.png](/images/blacklist-email.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 4">
    ## Confirm Blacklisting

    After entering the email, click "Add" to finalize the action. The system will then add the email to the blacklist, preventing any future bookings from that client.

    ![images/blacklist-clients.png](/images/blacklist-clients.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Next Steps

Now that you've blacklisted a client, you can manage your blacklist entries, remove restrictions if necessary, or proceed with importing client lists for bulk management.
