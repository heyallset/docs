## Introduction

Set up your location and tax info to ensure customers can find you and receive accurate pricing at checkout. This helps streamline bookings and keeps your business compliant.

Follow the steps below to get started

Step 1 - Go to location <img height="360" width="570" alt="Location Gif" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/G3UeoRgeJ1LBZUCv5ZIdI58wy8U.gif" />
To add a new location, go to '[**Business Settings**](https://app.heyallset.com/settings)' and click on '[**Location**](https://app.heyallset.com/settings?path=location).' From there, you can easily add and set up a new location for your business.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Step 2 - Enter location related info

<img height="367" width="700" alt="Contact" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/D6aWwodwwYKfn0iKl8B6IXP0Pg.png" />

Make sure to enter all your operating locations so customers can easily find you. Also, provide accurate tax information to stay compliant and ensure it appears correctly on customer receipts.

## Next Steps

Once you've set up your location, it's time to create the services your business offers. Adding your services allows customers to see what you provide and book appointments easily.
