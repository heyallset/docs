## Introduction

Welcome to All Set, The simple tool to manage your business. Handle clients, schedule appointments, and take payments—all from one place. Easy, efficient, and ready to keep you on track.

## Sign up

<img height="367" width="700" alt="Sign Page" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/QSKRAWXdBuUbi875IBQ8dFshqBw.png" />

Sign up with your business email for secure access and make sure to use a complex password. A strong password keeps your account safe—mix letters, numbers, and special characters to protect your business info.

## Onboarding - Contact info

<img height="367" width="700" alt="Contact" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/QKw3mT7UhdWN8562RdhDRZQ5YNE.png" />

Enter your business name as you want it to appear on your booking portal—this is how clients will recognize you. Ensure you provide a valid website address, as this is what clients will use to reach your services.

## Onboarding - Business size

<img height="367" width="700" alt="Business Size" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/FVXbAYr2t8ucKjjjLcs5b1Dfs.png" />
Enter the size of your business so we can tailor the setup to fit your needs and maximize efficiency. This helps us customize features and recommendations that work best for your operations.

## Onboarding - API Keys

<img height="367" width="700" alt="API key" src="https://framerusercontent.com/images/YQdbmk4IYab6mcp36TQggA94L8Q.png" />

Enter your Payment Provider's API keys to enable secure transactions. Please note that All Set is currently available only for select merchants.

## Next Steps

Once you complete your onboarding, you can start setting up your business details to get your booking portal ready. This allows customers to easily book appointments and access your services right away.
