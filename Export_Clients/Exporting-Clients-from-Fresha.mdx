## Introduction

Exporting your client list from Fresha gives you a complete overview of your customer data. Whether you're switching to All Set or simply backing up your records, you can export your list in just a few steps—on both desktop and mobile.

## On Desktop

![Export Your Client List Web Fresha Jpe](/images/Export_your_client_list_web_Fresha.jpeg)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1">
    ## Open Your Clients List

    Log into your Fresha account on desktop, then go to the Clients section from the main menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2">
    ##  Review Duplicate Profiles

    At the top of the client list, Fresha will flag any duplicate profiles. Click Review & merge to combine them before exporting to ensure clean data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3">
    ## Export the List

    Click the Options menu in the top right corner and select CSV format to download the file to your device. This ensures your client data is formatted properly for import into All Set.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## On Mobile

![Export Your Client Lis Mob Fresha Jpe](/images/Export_your_client_lis_mob_Fresha.jpeg)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1">
    ## Open Your Clients List

    Open the Fresha mobile app and navigate to the Clients tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2">
    ## Review Duplicate Profiles

    If duplicates are detected, a purple banner will appear at the top. Tap Review & merge to clean up the data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3">
    ## Export the List

    Tap the Actions menu (three dots) in the top-right corner and select Export your client list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 4">
    ## Choose File Format & Download

    Select CSV format and download the file to your mobile device. This ensures your client data is formatted properly for import into All Set.
  </Step>
</Steps>
