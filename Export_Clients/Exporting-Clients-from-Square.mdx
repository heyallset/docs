## Introduction

Exporting your clients from Square lets you easily take your customer information—such as names, phone numbers, and emails—with you as you transition to a new platform.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1">
    ## Log into Your Square Dashboard

    Go to [<u>squareup.com</u>](http://squareup.com) and sign in to your Square Dashboard using your business login.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2">
    ## Navigate to Customers

    From the main dashboard, click on Customers in the left-hand menu. This will bring you to your full client list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3">
    ## Click the Export Button

    On the top right corner of the Customers page, you’ll see an Import/Export button. Click it and select Export Customers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 4">
    ## Choose Export Format

    Square will export your customer list as a CSV file. Before importing this file into another platform, make sure the CSV is saved using comma-separated values (",")—not semicolon-separated (";"). This ensures your data maps correctly when importing elsewhere.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 5">
    ## Save the File

    Download and save the file to your computer. It includes key client details like names, phone numbers, emails, and notes
  </Step>
</Steps>
